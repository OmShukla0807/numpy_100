
How do we look for a function name?
dir(np): give list of functions->
example:['__version__', 'show_config', 'array', 'zeros', ...]

use pattern matching in this to obtain the name of a function.
suppose you want to know whether some function related to config exist or not?
input:[x for x in dir(np) if "config" in x.lower()]
output:['show_config']

how do we get to know about a function?
you can read the docstring related to a function: for example once you get to know that your function name is show_config
print(np.show_config.__doc__)
